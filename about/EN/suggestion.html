Advices to the buyers

  • It is not easy to choose your desired apartment. Be very serious about it.
  • Avoid the mistakes that result from inexperience and hurry so that you feel satisfied with your choice.
  • Obtain precise schedule of construction; make sure that you ask about the dates of completion so that you ensure trustworthiness of the selected construction.
  • Ask the mechanisms of quality control used by the company during construction.
  • Do not be lazy. Visit your future apartment during the construction process often; compare the parameters of the built apartment to the drawing.
  • Find out when the power, gas, elevator will be activated.
  • Always check whether the seller is the owner or legal agent of the property (check www.reestri.gov.ge).
  • Check whether there are any mortgages, indebtedness and any other obligation registered on this property (check www.reestri.gov.ge)
  • All arrangements must be followed by a contract so that the parties to the agreement are protected.
