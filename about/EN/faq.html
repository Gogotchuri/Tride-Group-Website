Payment specifications

Which conditions must be met for the internal credit and what kind of documentation must be presented?

For internal credit you need to present your ID card and the amount of first instalment – 30% of total cost.

Is it possible to apply for bank credit?

Bank credit is possible. “Tride” ensures conclusion of required documentation with the partner bank and approval of mortgage loan with the discounted terms of memorandum on cooperation. The collateral shall be the real estate that will be purchased.

Is the internal credit possible?

Internal credit is possible with the schedule and terms suited for the client.

Which banks does the Company cooperate with?

The company “Tride Group” cooperates with “TBC” and “VTB” Banks.

Is there a discount in case of full one-time payment?

In case of full one-time payment there is a discount which decreases the current cost of 1 sq. m. by $10.

Projects of the complex, design

How many apartments are there in the project of “Tride”?

6 buildings of the complex are already settled where there are up to 700 families residing. Totally, the complex of “Tride” is suited for more than 1000 families.

Totally how many buildings are there in the residential complex of “Tride”?

Today 5 buildings of the complex are constructed, put into operation and settled: “A”, “B”, “C” sections, “Green House”, “Panorama 1”, “Panorama 2”; “Tride Panorama 3” is being constructed and the sales have started from 1 November 2016.

What are the areas of apartments in the project of “Tride”?

In the project of “Tride” you can choose an apartment of any area above 45 sq. m.

Is there internet and other communications at the territory of the complex?

There are all forms of communications at the territory of residential complex of “Tride”.

Are the underground parking places considered in the buildings?

Yes, underground parking lots are considered in our project.

How many square meters is the “Tride” complex holding; what is the area of buildings-structures and recreational zone?

Construction site of the complex includes the territory of 33 500 sq. meters out of which 28 800 sq. meters will remain as recreational zone after completion of construction.

Are the buildings seismically stable?

In “Dighomi Park” all construction requirements set out by the legislation are met. At the construction territory we have carried out geophysical examinations to determine strength and seismic stability. Seismic strength of the buildings of “Dighomi Park” is 8 points.

Do you perform expertise to the construction materials?

The concrete and reinforcement at each floor is being examined during the construction process in Levan Samkharauli National Forensics Bureau. In case of desire the client can request and receive the respective conclusions.

What type of framework structure is used?

All buildings of “Dighomi Park” are constructed on the massive “pillow” and reinforced concrete frame is used, diaphragms of vertical rigidity.
